[h1]More Utility Armors[/h1]

You want to stack more utilities on your pawns? Why can a pawn have Jump pack, armor and not something else? Well, now they can!

[h2]Features[/h2]

[list]
    [*]This mod adds more armor and helmet variants with utility functions.
[/list]

[h3]Skunk Armor[/h3]

Recon armor with built-in Smokepop, available for crafting when you research both Smokepop and Recon armor.

[h3]Iudash Helmet[/h3]

Recon helmet with built-in Shard insanity lance, available for crafting when you research both Insanity weaponry and Recon armor.

[h3]Dragonslayer Armor[/h3]

Marine armor with built-in Firefoam popper, available for crafting when you research both Firefoam and Marine armor.

[h3]Abductor Helmet[/h3]

Marine armor helmet with built-in Shard shock lance, available for crafting when you research both Insanity weaponry and Marine armor.

[h3]Palisade Armor[/h3]

Cataphract armor with built-in shield, available for crafting when you research both Shields and Cataphract armor.

[h3]Shelter Armor[/h3]

Cataphract armor with built-in deployable low-shield bubble, available for crafting when you research both Shields and Cataphract armor.

[h3]Pharos Helmet[/h3]

Cataphract armor helmet with built-in disruptor flare launcher, available for crafting when you research both Disruptor flares and Cataphract armor.
