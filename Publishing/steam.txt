
[h1]More Utility Armors[/h1]

You want to stack more utilities on your pawns? Why can pawn have Jump pack armor and not something else? Well, now they can!

[h2]Features[/h2] 

[list]
    [*]This mods adds more armor variants with utility function.
[/list]

[h3]Skunk Armor[/h3] 

Recon armor with builtin smokepop, avialable for crafting when you research both smokepop and recon armor.

[h3]Iudash helmet[/h3]

Recon helmet with builtin shard insanity lance, avialable for crafting when you research both insanity weaponry and recon armor.

[h3]Dragonslayer Armor[/h3]

Marine armor with builtin firefoam popper, avialable for crafting when you research both firefoam and marine armor.

[h3]Abductor Helmet[/h3]

Marine armor helmet with builtin shard shock lance, avialable for crafting when you research both insanity weaponry and marine armor.

[h3]Palisade Armor[/h3]

Cataphract armor with builtin shield, avialable for crafting when you research both shields and cataphract armor. 

[h3]Pharos Helmer[/h3]

Cataphract armor with builtin disruptor flare launger, avialable for crafting when you research both disruptor flares and cataphract armor.
